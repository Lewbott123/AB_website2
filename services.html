Air Conditioning

▾

Keep your vehicle cool, comfortable, and efficient with our professional air conditioning services. Whether your system isn’t blowing cold air, has an unpleasant smell, or hasn’t been serviced in a while, our technicians can help.

We offer both R-134a and R-1234yf gases. Here is a brief guide to tell which gas your vehicle uses:

  • R-134a: Became the standard for vehicles manufactured from 1994 until 2016 to reduce environmental impact. If your vehicle was manufactured between 1994 and 2016 it is likely to use this gas.
  • R-1234yf: Became the standard for vehicles manufactured after 2016 to reduce environmental impact even further. If your vehicle was manufactured after 2016 it is likley to use this gas.

Why service your air conditioning?

  • Improves cooling performance
  • Eliminates odours and bacteria
  • Enhances fuel efficiency
  • Extends the lifespan of the overall system

Tyres & Alignment

▾

Your tyres are the only point of contact between your vehicle and the road, making them critical for safety, handling, and fuel efficiency. Our tyre services ensure you stay safe and in control in all driving conditions.

We offer a full range of tyre services, including:

  • Tyre fitting and replacement
  • Wheel balancing
  • Puncture repairs
  • Tyre pressure checks and adjustments
  • 4 wheel laser alignment (tracking)

Why are well-maintained tyres important?

  • Improves grip and handling
  • Enhances braking performance
  • Increases fuel efficiency
  • Reduces uneven wear and extends tyre life

Cambelts & Wet Belts

▾

Not sure when your cambelt was last changed? It’s one of the most important parts of your engine and one of the easiest to forget.

If it fails, it can lead to major (and expensive) damage. That’s why we offer reliable cambelt and wet belt replacements to keep your vehicle running smoothly and avoid unexpected breakdowns.

We’ll advise you on the right time to replace it based on your vehicle and mileage.
